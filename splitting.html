6LB Maul Versus 8LB Maul

What is the difference:

6LB Maul
Pros: Light weight, Easier to manage, Ability to be more accurate/precise, Won't tire as fast
Cons: Requires more hits to split the round

8LB Maul
Pros: Easier to split larger rounds, Less hits to split
Cons: Heavy to manage for some, Depending on technique can be less accurate
